Chocolate Cupcake Recipe

Chocolate cupcakes

Description

Delicious Chocolate cupcakes for your next party

Ingredients

  • Baking Soda
  • Baking Powder
  • Cocoa Powder
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Vanilla/li>

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a muffin pan with paper or foil liners. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa and salt. Set side
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time
  4. then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Add the flour mixture alternately with the milk; beat well. Fill the muffin cups 3/4 full
