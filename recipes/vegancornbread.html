Vegan Cornbread

Description

Super Moist Vegan Cornbread Recipe

Ingredients

  • Soy Milk
  • Sugar
  • Vegetable Shortening
  • Salt
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Baking Powder

Description

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a 9x5-inch baking pan with cooking spray.
  2. Beat 1/2 cup soy milk, sugar, vegetable shortening, salt, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl until well blended.
  3. Mix flour, cornmeal, and baking powder together in a bowl.
  4. Stir flour mixture into sugar mixture
  5. Alternating with the remaining 1 cup soy milk. Beat until well blended
  6. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes.
